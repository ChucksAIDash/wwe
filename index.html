Texture guide

Refractor
Standard chrome rainbow sheen. Clean, no pattern overlay. The baseline.
Mini-Diamond Mega
Tiny repeating diamond lattice across the whole card surface.
RayWave Value
Wavy ray-burst lines radiating outward. Retro-energy look.
Geometric Delight
Bold geometric shapes overlaid on the design. Hard angles, high contrast.
Diamond Plate Value
Metallic industrial diamond-plate texture. Steel floor grating on chrome.
X-Fractor Mega
Classic crosshatch X-pattern. A long-standing Chrome staple.
Logofractor WM Ed.
WWE logo embedded as a repeating foil pattern. Even the base looks premium.
Prism Hobby
Oil-slick rainbow prismatic effect. Full spectrum color shift by angle.
Negative Hobby
Photo colors fully inverted — skin tones go dark, backgrounds go light.
Sonar Hobby
Concentric ring waves from a center point. Radar / sonar pulse look.
Steel Cage Hobby
Wire cage/mesh texture overlaid. WWE-themed, very short print odds.
FrozenFractor Hobby /5
Ice-crystal fractal pattern, cold blue tone. Among the rarest Hobby numbered.
Notes

Logofractor Edition sold at WrestleMania 42 (April 2026). Its numbered rainbow runs Green /99 → Gold /50 → Orange /25 → Black /10 → Red /5 → Rose Gold 1/1, skipping the /250–/150 stops used in the main set.

Mini-Diamond rainbow skips Green: Pink /250 → Blue /150 → Purple /75 → Gold /50 → Orange /25 → Black /10 → Red /5.
